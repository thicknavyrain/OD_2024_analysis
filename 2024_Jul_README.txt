Column name: "directory_name_rectified"
Some of the directory name had spaces at the begining, ending & in-between causing inconvience while matching. So they were trimmed & all charaters were converted to uppercase.

Column name: "view"
Field of vision of many camera had occlusion. So they are tagged accordingly with tags clear & occluded. Useful for sensitivity analysis.

Column name: "datetime_rectified"
Many images had wrong timestamp & they are rectified.
